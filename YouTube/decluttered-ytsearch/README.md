# Decluttered YouTube Search

A lightweight UserScript that restores simplicity to YouTube's search results by giving you control over what you see. Hide distracting content, unwanted Shorts, and already-watched videos via a clean, integrated menu.

![Script Preview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dv-001/userscripts/refs/heads/master/YouTube/decluttered-ytsearch/assets/preview.webp)

## Features

* **Integrated Settings:** A gear icon next to the search bar lets you access your filters instantly.
* **Live Hover Preview:** See exactly what a filter does before applying it. Hovering over a menu option highlights the affected content on the page.
* **Hide Clutter:** Remove "For You," "People also watched," and other distracting content shelves.
* **Filter Shorts:** Hide dedicated Shorts grids and individual Shorts scattered within results.
* **Hide Watched Videos:** Automatically hide videos past a specific watch percentage (default is 90%).
* **Lightweight & Persistent:** Uses efficient CSS injection rather than heavy page scanning. Settings save automatically for future visits.

## Quick Start

1. Perform any search on YouTube.
2. Click the new **gear icon** located to the right of the search bar.
3. Hover over the filters to see what they hide, then toggle your preferences.
4. Click anywhere outside the panel to automatically save and close.
